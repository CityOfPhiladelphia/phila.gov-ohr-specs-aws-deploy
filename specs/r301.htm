CITY OF PHILADELPHIA RECREATION DEPARTMENT

R301
Recreation Specialty Instructor

This is an EXEMPT position.

GENERAL DEFINITION

An instructor in one of a variety of recreation skills or specialties at a municipal facility. Work involves the preparation of course material and its formal presentation to an organized group(s). Conitnuous employment is limited to a period of nine calendar months from date of appointment in any 12-month period.
