CITY OF PHILADELPHIA PERSONNEL DEPARTMENT

5H03
CORRECTIONAL OFFICER TRAINEE

Specialties
( Bilingual ) ( General )

GENERAL DEFINITION

This is correctional work of a training nature. Employees in this class participate as learners under the instruction of other security personnel in a correctional institution or police detention facility enforcing a wide variety of rules, regulations and disciplinary measures with successful completion of classroom course work and/or on-the-job correctional assignments required before candidates are eligible for promotion to the Correctional Officer class. Work is performed under the close supervision of higher level correctional or police personnel. Work involves regular exposure to unpredictable conditions, and occasionally requires the expenditure of physical effort in restraining and subduing inmates.

ALLOCATING FACTORS: (The following conditions must be met for a position to be approved for this class.)

  • The employee must participate in a classroom and/or on-the-job training program in preparation for promotion to Correctional Officer.

TYPICAL EXAMPLES OF WORK (ILLUSTRATIVE ONLY)

BILINGUAL SPECIALTY

  • Performs the duties below with members of the English language speaking and designated non- English language speaking populations.
BOTH SPECIALTIES

  • Participates in a training program to maintain security and discipline in a City correctional institution; attends classes; reads a variety of literature and reports on correctional programs; accompanies a Correctional Officer who patrols an assigned area of an institution such as cell blocks and dormitories; observes the manner in which he/she conducts roll calls, and inspects inmates for personal cleanliness and adherence to institutional rules and regulations; participates in a wide range of correctional activities under the guidance of full performance correctional personnel including such duties as escorting inmates throughout the institution, assisting in maintaining order in yards, mess halls and recreation areas.
  • Participates in a training program to maintain security in a police detention unit; receives on-the- job training and observes full performance security personnel engaged in receiving, recording, searching and processing prisoners; receives instruction in the operation of various electronic and computerized equipment used to record prisoner information, fingerprints and images; receives instruction in the identification and processing of forms and legal documentation; participates in escorting prisoners, distributing meals to prisoners and providing security in preliminary arraignments.
  • Performs a myriad of other activities in the correctional or detention field designed to qualify the enrollee as a full performance Correctional Officer.
  • Performs related work as required.

REQUIRED KNOWLEDGES, SKILLS AND ABILITIES

ABILITY TO::

  • learn the attitudes and group habits of person in custody.
  • learn and enforce a wide variety of correctional institution or detention facility rules, regulations and disciplinary measure with firmness and impartiality.
  • learn the health and safety precautions associated with correctional work.
  • supervise the activities of inmates in assigned tasks.
  • speak, read, and write in a specified non-English language, and the English language, fluently for the Bilingual Specialty.
  • and willingness to carry out detailed orders.
  • establish and maintain effective working relationships with inmates and associates.
  • learn the operation of, and to attain proficiency with a variety of firearms, and to maintain such proficiency during tenure as a Correctional Officer Trainee if required by work assignment.
  • act quickly and calmly in an emergency.
DESIRABLE TRAINING AND EXPERIENCE

BOTH SPECIALTIES

  • Education equivalent to completion of the twelfth school grade.
BILINGUAL SPECIALTY

  • Sufficient training and/or experience to be able to understand and speak fluently and clearly in English and a designated non-English language.
PHYSICAL AND MEDICAL REQUIREMENTS

  • Age - Must have reached twentieth (20) birthday.
  • Ability to physically perform the duties and to work in the environmental conditions required of a position in this class.
LICENSES, REGISTRATIONS AND/OR CERTIFICATES

  • Possession of a valid proper class motor vehicle operator's license as issued by the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania prior to appointment and during tenure as a Correctional Officer Trainee if required by work assignment.
