CITY OF PHILADELPHIA PERSONNEL DEPARTMENT

9D01
RECREATION ATTENDANT

GENERAL DEFINITION

This is routine manual and limited recreation work.

Employees in this class are responsible for assisting sub-professional and professional recreation personnel in conducting activities at a recreation facility. Emphasis is on duties which will facilitate the work of higher level personnel, such as dispensing equipment and supplies or supervising free play, but employees in this class may occasionally assist in conducting organized activities. All work is performed in accordance with specific instructions, and employees are subject to frequent supervision from a professional recreation worker.

TYPICAL EXAMPLES OF WORK (ILLUSTRATIVE ONLY)

  • Prepares and dispenses recreation equipment and supplies; participates in making minor repairs to equipment and in marking courts, diamonds, and fields.
  • Supervises free play and use of swings, slides, sand boxes, and wading pools.
  • Assists in conducting organized recreation activities and in officiating sports and games.
  • Receives phone calls and gives information concerning program and schedule at the recreation area; maintains records of competitive athletic activities.
  • Performs related work as required.

REQUIRED KNOWLEDGES, SKILLS AND ABILITIES

KNOWLEDGE OF:

  • and ability to participate in one or more group recreational activities.

ABILITY TO:

  • establish and maintain effective working relationships with patrons of various age groups and backgrounds, and the general public.
  • cooperate and work effectively with recreation staff members.
  • understand and follow oral and written instructions.

EXPERIENCE AND TRAINING

(The following statement represents the minimum training and experience standards which will be used to admit or reject applicants for tests.)
  • Education equivalent to completion of three years of high school.

Or any equivalent combination of acceptable experience and training.
