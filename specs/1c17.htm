CITY OF PHILADELPHIA PERSONNEL DEPARTMENT

1C17
PHOTOTYPESETTER OPERATOR


GENERAL DEFINITION

This is specialized work operating an automated typesetting machine in the preparation of master copies. Employees in this class use machine software to compose forms layouts for reproduction purposes. Work is performed under the supervision of a technical superior.


TYPICAL EXAMPLES OF WORK (ILLUSTRATIVE ONLY)

  • Types, using automated typesetting equipment, from drafts, roughs, or preprinted material.
  • Operates automated typesetting equipment via typewriter-oriented keyboard; uses software programs to select type fonts, point and pica size, edit type sizing and line spacing, justify margins, and set vertical rules; views format on interactive display screen; proofreads completed work to correct errors or revise formats.
  • Stores, files, and merges compositions on diskettes; codes stored material for access.
  • Performs related work as required.


REQUIRED KNOWLEDGES, SKILLS AND ABILITIES

KNOWLEDGE OF:

  • the uses and capacities of software programs and their associated non-procedural language for automated typesetting equipment.
  • capacities and features of automated typesetting accessory equipment.
  • the capacities and features of automated typesetting equipment.
  • business English, spelling, and arithmetic.

SKILL IN:

  • the operation of an automated typesetting machine and keyboard proficiency.

ABILITY TO:

  • learn the standards of composition used in the preparation of forms.
  • understand and follow oral and written instructions.
  • perform work rapidly and accurately.
  • recognize and correct grammar and diction errors.


MINIMUM ACCEPTABLE TRAINING AND EXPERIENCE

(The following statement represents the minimum training and experience standards which will be used to admit or reject applicants for tests. Applications submitted by candidates for this class will be reviewed based on training and experience requirements as approved on 5/1987.)

  • Education equivalent to completion of the twelfth school grade and completion of a training program in the use of automated typesetting machinery.
  • One year of clerical work which has included the operation of automated typesetting machinery.

Or any equivalent combination of education and experience determined to be acceptable by the Personnel Department.

PHYSICAL AND MEDICAL REQUIREMENTS

  • Ability to physically perform the duties and to work in the environmental conditions required of a position in this class.
