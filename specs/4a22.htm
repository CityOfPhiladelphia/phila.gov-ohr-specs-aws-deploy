CITY OF PHILADELPHIA PERSONNEL DEPARTMENT

4A22
ELECTROCARDIOGRAPH TECHNICIAN

GENERAL DEFINITION

This is technical work in the operation of electrocardiograph equipment. Work is performed under the supervision of a higher level electrocardiograph technician. Assignments are stable in nature and are carried out in accordance with instructions and established routines. The employee in this class is responsible for taking an adequate electrocardiogram tracing. Conditions involve some disagreeable aspects and light physical effort.

TYPICAL EXAMPLES OF WORK (ILLUSTRATIVE ONLY)

  • Attaches electrodes to specified areas of patient's ankles and wrists; connects leads from the electrocardiograph to the electrodes.
  • Turns on power and manipulates selector switch to secure tracings of electromotive variations in action of specific areas of heart muscles.
  • Identifies sections of tracings by use of markers; cuts tracings into appropriate sections; mounts tracings.
  • Calculates orthodiagrams and posts them to ward reports.
  • Keeps electrocardiograph in working order by replenishing supply of tracing paper and ink.
  • Performs related work as required.

REQUIRED KNOWLEDGES, SKILLS AND ABILITIES

KNOWLEDGE OF:

  • the principles of operation of an electrocardiograph.

SKILL IN:

  • the operation and care of an electrocardiograph and to recognize defective operation.

ABILITY TO:

  • recognize abnormal wave patterns.
  • gain and retain the confidence and cooperation of patients undergoing tests and to handle disturbed patients safely and sympathetically.
  • understand and effectively carry out complex oral and written instructions.

MINIMUM ACCEPTABLE TRAINING AND EXPERIENCE

(The following statement represents the minimum training and experience standards which will be used to admit or reject applicants for tests.)
  • Education equivalent to the completion of the twelfth school grade.
  • Completion of a course of training in the operation of an electrocardiograph under the supervision of a cardiologist or experienced technician.
PHYSICAL AND MEDICAL REQUIREMENTS

  • Ability to physically perform the duties and to work in the environmental conditions required of a position in this class.
