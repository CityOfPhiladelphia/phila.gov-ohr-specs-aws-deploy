Job Interest

Job Interest Form

See a Civil Service Job Class Specification listed but cannot find an open job announcement for it? Submit a job interest form to the City of Philadelphia, Office of Human Resources and be notified when a specific job title is going to be announced. Search for the job class specification (job title) below and click on the () on the row of the job title you are interested in. This will take you to the Job Interest Form. There you can fill out contact information and be notified via email when the City of Philadelphia is announcing that specific job title.

Disclaimer

The City of Philadelphia will attempt to contact you via e mail when this job opportunity is open for applications, however the City assumes no liability for misdirected email, undelivered email flagged as spam or other email delivery problems or issues. Job postings are updated every two weeks and potential candidates are encouraged to visit our website frequently to see if a particular job posting is open for applications.
