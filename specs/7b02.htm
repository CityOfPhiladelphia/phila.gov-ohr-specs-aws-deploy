CITY OF PHILADELPHIA PERSONNEL DEPARTMENT

7B02
WATER DISTRIBUTION REPAIR WORKER

GENERAL DEFINITION

This is field water distribution repair and maintenance work at the full performance level. Incumbents perform a variety of assignments required by the repair and maintenance of water mains and equipment. Employees in this class may work evening, weekends, and holidays as assignments require. Work is performed under the close supervision of field crew chiefs. An employee in this class performs firstline repair and maintenance of segments of the city-wide water distribution system. Work involves the exertion of moderate physical effort under conditions which can be only partially controlled.

TYPICAL EXAMPLES OF WORK (ILLUSTRATIVE ONLY)

  • Installs sections of new water mains; locates and repairs leaks and breaks in piping; caulks pipe joints with packing and hot lead or straps a metal band around joint to prevent leakage. < li> Installs ferrules or valved sleeves in water mains using a manually or electrically operated ferrule installation machine.
  • Installs, tests, repairs and repacks water valves on water mains udder or with out pressure using hand tools; assists in flow studies by drilling taps and inserting gauges.
  • Installs, repairs and maintains fire hydrants using hand tools.
  • May drive a truck to and from work areas.
  • May supervise a small crew of employees consisting of an equipment operator, laborers and/or helpers.
  • Operates air-driven tools, such as a pneumatic drill to break had ground surfaces; digs and backfills dirt in work area.
  • Distributes tools to crew members and insures that tools and equipment are properly cared for and returned to the vehicle at the end of each repair task.
  • May notify customers of possible shutdowns or disruptions of service.
  • Performs related work as required.

REQUIRED KNOWLEDGES, SKILLS AND ABILITIES

KNOWLEDGE OF:

  • the methods, materials, tools and practices used in the installation, repair and maintenance of water mains and equipment.
  • the care and use of tools used in the work.
  • the hazards and safety precautions involved in the work.
  • the practices governing the operation of water distribution systems and their component parts.

SKILL IN:

  • the care and use of tools.

ABILITY TO:

  • supervise the work of a small crew of employees engaged in performing tasks to facilitate the installation, repair and maintenance of water mains and equipment.
  • maintain simple records.
  • write simple reports.
  • perform manual labor often under adverse climatic conditions.
  • understand and follow oral and written instructions.

MINIMUM ACCEPTABLE TRAINING AND EXPERIENCE

(The following statement represents the minimum training and experience standards which will be used to admit or reject applicants for tests. Applications submitted by candidates for this class will be reviewed based on training and experience requirements as approved on 3/72.)
  • Education equivalent to completion of the eighth school grade.
  • Two years of recent field laboring experience in the installation, repair or maintenance of water or gas distribution mains and equipment, one year of which has been above the laborer level.
Or any equivalent combination of education and experience determined to be acceptable by the Personnel Department.

PHYSICAL AND MEDICAL REQUIREMENTS

  • Ability to physically perform the duties and to work in the environmental conditions required of a position in this class.

LICENSES, REGISTRATIONS, AND/OR CERTIFICATES

  • Possession of a valid proper class motor vehicle operator's license as issued by the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania prior to appointment and during tenure of employment as a Water Distribution Repair Worker if required by work assignment.
